League of Legends

League of Legends is the world's best multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game.

It is a new genre of online game where you can enjoy team play and RTS and RPG in one game simultaneously through endless real-time combat and collaboration.

Both teams summon powerful champions, each with unique characteristics and style, to face off in different modes of battle.

Enjoy a thrilling battle with many fellow summoners in the League of Legends, where new champions are constantly added, updates are continuing, and exciting e-sports competitions are at the center!
